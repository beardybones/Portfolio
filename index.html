Welcome & Introduction

Hello, my name is Alan J. Lopez

I am a full stack web developer with an engine of curiosity that drives me. Working well with others and utilizing the technologies at my disposal has helped me go from leading my first store at 19 to opening and managing as GM through hard work and a will of determination for others to follow. My initial background was in the accounting field, from an oil company to one of the biggest turf companies, but coding has long been a passion of mine.
